About Our Salon


MBSAI is located in Karachi and is one of the best growing Salon of Pakistan. From humble beginning of renting a singular beauty room within a hair salon, Mehak Beauty Salon is now taking care of all of your needs.


Thanks to MBSAI Beauty faithfuls, the Salons are fast becoming the most recommended salons in Karachi, the founders are still very much involved in the background, and oversee the daily running of the Salons as well as providing regular Staff Training to ensure you, our beloved clients, are still the focal point of what we are.


Located at GULSHAN-E-IQBAL, MEHAK Beauty salon provides you a range of highly trendy hair cuts and styling, makeup, hair coloring, waxing services, spray tanning and facials. It has a team of highly skilled and experienced stylists.


Whether you want to get a simple, classic hair cut or you want to get a personalized look from the stylists, Mehak salon is for you. They offer a range of services starting from hair styling, hair coloring, makeup, eyelash extensions to skincare, bronzing, hair removal and barbering.
